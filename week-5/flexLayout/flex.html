CHARMINAR

The Capital City

The Hirakud Dam, located on the Mahanadi River in Odisha, India, is one of the world's longest earthen dams, measuring approximately 25.8 km including dykes. Inaugurated in 1957, it is a major multipurpose project for flood control, irrigation, and power generation, significantly boosting regional agriculture and electricity supply.
